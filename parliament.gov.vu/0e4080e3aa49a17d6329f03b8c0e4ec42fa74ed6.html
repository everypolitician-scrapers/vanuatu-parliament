Written by Vira Isno on 20 January 2015. Posted in Biography

Hon. Edwin Amblus Macreveth

Friend Melanesian Party (FMP)

Contact Address:
Parliament of Vanuatu

Vanuatu

Basic Information Consituency: Santo Rural Parliamentary Appointment:
