Hon. Andrew Solomon Napuat

Written by Vira Isno on 20 January 2015. Posted in Biography

Hon. Andrew Napuat

GJP

Contact Address:
Parliament of Vanuatu
PMB 9052
Port Vila
Vanuatu
Phone: +67833060 / +67822229
 
Basic Information Consituency: Tanna Parliamentary Appointment:
Elected to the Parliament of Vanuatu for Tanna Constituency in 2016 Parliamentary Committee:
 Ministerial Appointments:
Parliamentary Secretary - Internal Affairs. In-charge of Provincial Affairs and Police
 Education:
Master of Management (Currently doing final Thesis) at Revans University (IMCA - England), 2010 – 2013
Diploma in Business English & Letter Writing. (Currently undertaking) at Cambridge International College, 2007 -2008
Bachelor of Management. (Graduated with Distinction) at Revans University (IMCA - England), 2005–2006
Diploma in Human Resource/Personnel Management. (Graduated with Distinction) at Cambridge International College, 2005–2006
Diploma in Business (Frontline management). (Graduated) at Next (Vanuatu) Limited, 2002–2003
Year 12 Pacific Senior Secondary School Certificate (PSSC) at Matevulu College, 2000–2001 Occupation before entering Parliament:
National Advisory Board Secretariat – Ministry of Climate Change, 2015
First Political Advisor to the Minister of Lands, 2014 – 2015
Chief Executive Officer (CEO) to the President of the Republic, 2011 – 2014
Executive Officer/Human Resources Officer/Finance Officer (Ministry of Justice and Community services), 2009-2011
Senior Project Officer – Outreach (Youth Outreach Project) (Save the Children Australia), 2006-2008
Local Training Consultant (VATET PROJECT), 2005–2006
Training Coordinator/Facilitator (NEXT (Vanuatu) Ltd), 2003 –2005
 Year of birth: 21 March 1983 Home island: Tanna
